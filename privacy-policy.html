Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Tamil Radios.

Last updated: July 7, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Tamil Radios collects, uses, and protects information when visitors use this website.

Information We Collect

Tamil Radios is a free online radio directory and player. We do not ask visitors to create an account. When you use the website, basic technical information such as browser type, device type, pages visited, approximate location, date and time of visit, and referring pages may be collected automatically through server logs, analytics tools, cookies, or similar technologies.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to operate the website, improve page performance, understand which radio stations and pages are useful to visitors, prevent abuse, fix technical issues, and provide a better listening experience. We do not sell personal information.

Cookies and Advertising

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, measure traffic, and show advertisements. Google and other third-party vendors may use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google AdSense

We may display ads provided by Google AdSense. Google's advertising cookies help Google and its partners serve ads to visitors. Visitors can learn how Google uses data and manage ad personalization by visiting Google's Advertising Policies and Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

Radio streams, station logos, external links, analytics services, ad networks, and embedded media may be provided by third parties. These third parties may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Tamil Radios is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites and services.

Local Storage and Favorites

If you use the favorites feature, your selected stations may be stored locally in your browser on your own device. This information is used only to show your saved stations and is not submitted to us through an account system.

Children's Privacy

Tamil Radios is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this website, please contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Your Choices

You can disable or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt out of personalized advertising through Google Ads Settings or other available industry opt-out tools. Some website features may not work as expected if cookies or local storage are disabled.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect website changes, advertising requirements, legal requirements, or improvements to our services. The updated date at the top of this page shows when the policy was last revised.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, data practices, or advertising on Tamil Radios, please contact us through the Contact Us page.
