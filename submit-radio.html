Submit Radio

Submit your radio station.

Radio owners and station managers can send their station details to Tamil Radios. After review, approved stations will be listed in this app for listeners.

Send Your Details

Email your radio station information to mobiradion@gmail.com. Please use a clear subject such as "Submit Radio Station".

Required Information

Please include the station name, live stream URL, station logo, website link if available, language, country or city, and a short description of your radio station.

Review Process

We review each submission before adding it to Tamil Radios. Stations must have a working stream URL, suitable content, and accurate station information.

After Approval

If your station is approved, we will list it in the app so listeners can find and play your radio station. We may contact you by email if more details are needed.
