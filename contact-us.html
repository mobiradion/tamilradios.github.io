Contact Us

Get in touch with Tamil Radios.

For support, station updates, radio submissions, advertising questions, or general feedback, you can contact the Tamil Radios team by email.

Email

Please send your message to mobiradion@gmail.com. We will review your request and reply as soon as possible.

What to Include

If you are reporting a broken stream or submitting a radio station, include the station name, stream URL, website link, logo if available, and a short description so we can check it quickly.

Advertising and Partnerships

For advertising, partnership, or business-related questions, please mention your company or website name and the purpose of your request in the email.

Privacy Requests

If your message is related to privacy, cookies, or data questions, please include "Privacy Request" in the email subject so we can identify it clearly.
